Hello! My name is Hongyi Xie (谢泓毅). I will join the Asian School of the Environment at Nanyang Technological University as a Research Fellow.  

My research focuses on the constraints on low-carbon technology scale-up, and the environmental & cost implications of climate actions, with an emphasis on data-driven modeling and quantitative analysis. I am currently working on the development of the Global Change Analysis Model (GCAM) – Southeast Asia version.  

I am familiar with Life Cycle Assessment (LCA), Material Flow Analysis (MFA), Techno-Economic Analysis (TEA), energy systems modeling, and spatial analysis. My research has been published in journals including *Nature Sustainability*, *Engineering*, *Environmental Science & Technology*, and *Journal of Hydrology*. I served as an anonymous reviewer for several environmental journals, such as Nature Cities, ES&T, RCR, JCLP, JEMA.  

I hold a Ph.D. with honors in Environmental Management from Fudan University (2022-2026), an M.S. with honors in Environmental Science from Beijing Normal University (2017-2020), and a B.S. in Environmental Science from Zhejiang University (2013-2017). I was a Joint Ph.D. student in the National University of Singapore (2024–2025). Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as an Engineer at the Transport Planning and Research Institute, Ministry of Transport, China (2020–2022).
